Cewein 2020-1-10

Since and prior to https://github.com/Cewein/Spelunca/commit/25ab4df81cce811ace7e6d4f5a88abeec6cf7950 the world gen is halt the game when moving to new chunk. is might be due to the fact, as of now, the worldgen is single threaded.

there is two options to make it better and not halting the game :

  • [ ] make the gen of a chunk in a compute shader
  • [ ] make the gen of new chunks into another thread than the main one

for the easier time and having nothing to choose both will be implemented. please reference this issue if you commit something related to it.
